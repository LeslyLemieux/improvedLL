The following are the potential or suggested solutions for this problem:

1. One of the things we could do in order to break stereotyped gender roles is to equally give opportunities to both
men and women. This would mean that men could also do the jobs women do, and women can also do the jobs that men do.

2. Look for opportunities to raise awareness about stereotypes, whether they come from resources or from the person.

3. Do not set any gender expectations because this would only lead to more stereotypes. This would mean that we should
let the people be who they are and not tell them what they should or should not be.

4. Respect people and not judge. Even if we have different cultures/beliefs, one must not judge the other for the way
they act/are. This way, we can avoid further stereotyping.

5. Briefly educate people about it and how it affects people. The more people that know about it, the more they can
understand how it is wrong.
